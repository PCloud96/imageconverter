Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Cloud Motion Lab LLC ("we," "our," or "us") built Image Converter: Photo & File ("the App") as a commercial application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

100% Local Processing

All image conversions happen entirely on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to any server, cloud service, or external system. No image data ever leaves your iPhone. This is a core design principle of the App, not an afterthought.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require an account, login, or registration. Your conversion history, preferences, and purchase status are stored only on your device.

Photo Access

The App uses Apple's PHPicker to let you select specific photos for conversion. This grants the App access only to the photos you explicitly choose. We never request access to your full photo library. The App cannot browse, read, or modify any photos you have not selected.

Camera and Microphone

The App does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

Advertising

The App displays optional rewarded ads provided by Unity Ads. No forced or interstitial ads are shown. Rewarded ads are only shown when you choose to watch one to unlock a free conversion. Unity Ads may collect non-personal technical data (such as device model and OS version) to serve contextual ads. No user tracking is performed for ad targeting. For more information, see Unity's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases, including a weekly subscription and a lifetime access option, processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. All purchase management, including refunds and cancellations, is handled through your Apple ID account settings.

Data Storage

All app data, including conversion preferences, usage counts, and purchase status, is stored locally on your device. Converted images are saved to temporary storage on your device and are cleaned up automatically. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Export Watermark

Free users' exported images include a small watermark. This watermark is applied locally on your device during the export process. No data about your exports is collected or transmitted.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages and does not contain age-restricted content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Unity Ads — for displaying optional rewarded advertisements
Apple StoreKit — for processing in-app purchases

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
